# Articles

Find new papers, tools, techniques, releases, and thoughtful work that gives me a concrete idea.

Prefer primary sources, selected labs and artists from my Topics, strong novelty, and practical relevance to audiovisual work. Skip generic news, repeated announcements, listicles, and hype.

A long-running exhibition may appear once when it is better treated as cultural awareness than as a dated event. Do not include ordinary events, performances, screenings, openings, or calendar listings.
