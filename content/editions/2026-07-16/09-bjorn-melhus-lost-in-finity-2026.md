# Tonight: Bjørn Melhus turns the Betonhalle into a media circuit

> LOST IN FINITY opens free at silent green tonight, July 16, with doors at 19:00 and a welcome at 19:30; the exhibition then runs through August 23.

Bjørn Melhus has recombined material from 35 years of work into an immersive installation made for silent green’s Betonhalle. The venue describes a circuit through the media-shaped collective unconscious of the twentieth and twenty-first centuries, moving between religious imagery, pop-cultural end-time visions, collapse, and technological escape fantasies.

Tonight’s opening begins on the lawn at 19:00, followed by the welcome and admission to the installation at 19:30. Entry is free. If tonight is impractical, the exhibition is open Tuesday to Friday from 14:00 to 20:00 and Saturday to Sunday from 11:00 to 20:00 through August 23. A free artist talk with Melhus and curator Vanina Saracino takes place Friday at 19:00.

## Why this was selected

It is a purpose-built, time-based installation at a watched venue, opens tonight without booking friction, and remains available after the opening rush.
