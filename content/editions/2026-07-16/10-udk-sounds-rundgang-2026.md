# This weekend: one building full of new sonic work

> UdK Sound Studies opens its free SoundS Rundgang from July 17 to 19, with audiovisual and interactive installations, performances, and research spread through Lietzenburger Straße 45.

The Sound Studies and Sonic Arts programme organizes this year’s show around “Slack,” a refusal of enforced productivity in favor of pause, presence, and play. The main building includes works across its foyer, third-floor rooms, and courtyard, ranging from magnetic resonance and vibrotactile symbiosis to multichannel installation and performance.

The finite visit window is Friday 16:00–22:00, Saturday 11:00–22:00, or Sunday 10:00–19:00. Admission is free and no RSVP is listed. Go with one practical question rather than trying to absorb the entire Rundgang: which work makes its spatial or interactive rule legible within the first minute, and what does the sound do that the object or image could not do alone?
