# Stockhausen's helicopter quartet becomes an immersive Berlin opera

> Mittwoch aus Licht premieres at Deutsche Oper Berlin on September 19, 2026, with further performances on September 20, 23, 26, and 27. A free helicopter-quartet livestream precedes it on September 5 at 15:00.

Deutsche Oper Berlin is staging Karlheinz Stockhausen's Mittwoch aus Licht in the German-speaking world for the first time. Susanne Kennedy directs, Markus Selg leads stage design and video, Florent Derex handles sound design, and Maxime Pascal conducts. The production runs about four hours and combines live performance, spatial sound, video, and ritual-scale staging.

Its spectacular center is the Helicopter String Quartet: four musicians perform in four helicopters while their instrumental sound, rotor noise, and filmed images are transmitted back into the work. The opera premieres September 19 at 17:00, followed by performances September 20, 23, 26, and 27. The quartet itself flies earlier, with a free live broadcast at the Deutsche Oper on September 5 at 15:00. That broadcast is the low-commitment entry point; the full production is the deeper audiovisual study.

## Why this was selected

Few events expose the engineering of space, transmission, acoustic noise, live image, and composition at this scale.
