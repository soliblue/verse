# Reserve September 9 to 13 for Berlin Art Week

> Berlin Art Week runs September 9 through 13, 2026, with exhibitions, performances, film screenings, talks, gallery openings, project spaces, and collection visits across the city.

Berlin Art Week is less one event than a five-day routing problem. The 2026 edition opens across major institutions on Wednesday, September 9. Thursday adds Positions Berlin Art Fair at Tempelhof Airport and Gallery Night. Friday shifts attention toward independent initiatives and project spaces, Saturday opens selected private collections, and Sunday closes with artist talks, guided tours, and programming at the Berlin Art Week Garten.

The full program will determine what earns time, but the dates are firm: September 9 through 13, 2026. Do not try to cover the city. Once schedules appear, choose one institutional show, one independent space, and one performance or screening with a clear audiovisual component. The Transgressive Memory Festival at silent green runs on the final weekend, providing a useful anchor for that route.

## Why this was selected

It is the main citywide contemporary-art window of the autumn, and early calendar placement makes a focused route possible once the detailed program lands.
