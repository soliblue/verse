# Today in Berlin: seven rooms of digital work at P61

> Digital Dimensions runs at P61 Gallery from July 9, 2026 through March 13, 2027. Today, July 12, it opens at 14:00 at Potsdamer Straße 61.

P61 Gallery's Digital Dimensions brings together technology, video art, installations, and digital experiments across seven staged rooms. The official Berlin listing describes roughly 300 works and four video shows, with contributors including BALLZS7, MARTERIUM, Philipp Tur, Vincent Schwenk, Kristian Ozer Kettner, Junanjo McLittle, Escape Routine, and Finn Moeller.

The exhibition is an immediate field trip rather than a distant save-the-date. Its listed run is July 9, 2026 through March 13, 2027, and the July 12 session begins at 14:00. The venue is P61 Gallery, Potsdamer Straße 61, 10785 Berlin.
