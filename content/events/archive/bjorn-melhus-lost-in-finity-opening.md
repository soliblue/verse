# Bjørn Melhus: LOST IN FINITY opening

silent green opens an immersive installation built for the Betonhalle from newly recombined material spanning 35 years of Bjørn Melhus’s work.

## Why this was selected

It is a free, same-day opening at a watched venue and turns a large space into a time-based media circuit rather than a conventional screening.
