# DayDreamLab by Transmission

Transmission brings an all-ages rooftop gathering shaped by rave care, social justice, underground club history, eclectic DJ sets, and low-pressure community time.

## Why this was selected

It treats club culture as participatory community infrastructure and makes the rooftop accessible to families and curious newcomers, not only a standard night crowd.
