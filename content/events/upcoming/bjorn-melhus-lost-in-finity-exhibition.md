# Bjørn Melhus: LOST IN FINITY

An immersive Betonhalle installation recombines 35 years of video, sampled voices, projections, and sound into a circuit about crisis and imagined endings.

## Why this was selected

It is a free, time-based media installation built specifically for a large industrial space.
