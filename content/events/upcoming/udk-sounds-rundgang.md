# SoundS Rundgang: Slack

The UdK Sound Studies and Sonic Arts program opens a dense student exhibition of audiovisual and interactive installations, sound research, and performances around the theme of pause, presence, and play.

## Why this was selected

It is free, finite, and unusually concentrated: one visit can surface several practical ideas about spatial sound, interaction, installation, and performance.
