# Sonic Pluriverse: Mehdi Nassouli and Gnawa Diffusion

Mehdi Nassouli’s guembri-led set opens a rooftop evening that moves into Gnawa Diffusion’s politically charged blend of Gnawa, reggae, dub, rock, chaabi, and hip-hop.

## Why this was selected

It offers a strong rhythmic and diasporic counterpoint to the weekend’s technology-heavy media-art choices.
