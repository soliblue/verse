# silent green Open Lab #7

Shu and Frieda turn a 1990s Cantonese ballad into unstable sound research about migration and memory; GLTR Mauer follows with music shaped by shoegaze, game soundtracks, hardcore, hyperpop, and house.

## Why this was selected

It is free, artist-led, and close to the operator’s positive taste for independent, social work that can still surprise.
