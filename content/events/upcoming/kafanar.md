# Sky Sounds with KafaNar

Deniz Mahir Kartal connects Kaval, Duduk, Çağlama, Anatolian vocal traditions, loops, and rhythm in a rooftop solo performance. LEFEU plays before and after the live set.

## Why this was selected

The meeting of traditional instruments, electronics, diaspora experience, and a rooftop setting is unusually close to the core Verse taste profile.
