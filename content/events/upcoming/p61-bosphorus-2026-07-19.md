# What Time Is [Your] Color? - Bosphorus: A Field of Becoming

Faruk Bil and computational artist Calin Segal turn nearly 10,000 photographs of the Bosphorus surface into an interactive installation across P61's displays, with music by O.Bee.

## Why this was selected

It combines computational image-making, interaction, and sound at a watched digital-art venue.
