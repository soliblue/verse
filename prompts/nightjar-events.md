# Event guidance

Use primary venue or organizer pages. Verify the Berlin-local date and time, venue, address, price, booking state, and cancellation or sold-out state. If a fact is unknown, mark it unknown instead of guessing.

Keep all verified events in `content/events/upcoming/`; only the strongest time-sensitive events may become edition stories, with at most two per edition. Preserve series and occurrence ids, evidence URLs, checked timestamps, and story relations. Move ended events to the archive.

Keep event copy plain: what it is, when, where, and why it may matter. An edition event story uses `event` for `kind` and `nightjar-events-v1` as `prompt_version`. Do not turn listings into recommendations without evidence. Never store private locations or personal routing anchors.
